DEAN OF FACULTY

Professor Olubayo Oluduro

LL.M [Lagos], M.Phil [Ife], MBA [Ado Ekiti], Ph.D [Ghent]
Senior Fellow | Human Rights, Peace and Conflict Studies

Professor Oluduro is the Senior Fellow at the OGEES Institute and an Associate Editor of the Institute's Journal. He oversees interdisciplinary research exploring the intersections between oil exploration, human rights, and conflicts.

Called to the Nigerian Bar in 1996, he holds a PhD in oil and gas law from Ghent University, Belgium. He is an Associate of the Chartered Institute for Arbitration, UK, and has published widely on environmental and human rights issues.
