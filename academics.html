Academic Staff Directory

DEAN OF FACULTY

Professor Olubayo Oluduro

LL.M [Lagos], M.Phil [Ife], MBA [Ado Ekiti], Ph.D [Ghent]

Professor Oluduro is a Senior Fellow at the OGEES Institute and an expert in Environmental Law and Human Rights. He oversees interdisciplinary research exploring the intersections between oil exploration, ecological justice, and conflicts in Africa.
