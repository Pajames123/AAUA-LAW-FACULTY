LATEST ACHIEVEMENT

AAUA Law Wins 2025 FLAM Moot Court Competition

In a stunning display of legal prowess, the Adekunle Ajasin University Faculty of Law has emerged champions of the 2025 Football Law Annual Moot (FLAM) held in Abuja.

Competing against 44 universities from 7 countries, our team faced the University of Lagos (UNILAG) in the grand finale and secured victory with their superior arguments on "Contractual Stability in Football."

Welcome from the Dean

"It is my distinct privilege to welcome you to the Faculty of Law, Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba-Akoko (AAUA)."

As one of the flagship faculties in this 21st-Century University, we are driven by a singular mandate: to foster a culture of legal excellence, critical thinking, and unwavering integrity. Here, we do not merely teach the law; we mold the custodians of justice and the social engineers of tomorrow.

Our curriculum is robust and dynamic, designed to bridge the gap between theoretical legal frameworks and the practical realities of the Nigerian and global legal systems. From Public Law to Commercial jurisprudence, our distinguished faculty members are committed to imparting knowledge that stands the test of time.

To our prospective students: you are applying to join a community of scholars where character is valued as highly as learning. To our current students: continue to strive for the highest standards of the Bar. And to our alumni: you remain our proudest ambassadors in the legal marketplace.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our academic programs, our vibrant law society, and our contributions to legal research. Welcome to the Citadel of Legal Learning.

Professor Olubayo Oluduro

Dean, Faculty of Law
