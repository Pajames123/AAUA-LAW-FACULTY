Akungba Law Clinic

Bridging the gap between the law and the people. We provide free legal support, mediation, and counseling to the Akungba-Akoko community.

Get Legal Help For Students

Clinical Legal Education

The Akungba Law Clinic is not just a service center; it is a training ground. Under the strict supervision of experienced faculty staff and practicing attorneys, our law students apply legal theory to real-life problems.

We believe in Social Justice and Access to Law for the indigent and vulnerable members of society.

  • Confidentiality Guaranteed
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)
  • Human Rights Advocacy

Our Practice Areas

Mediation & ADR

We help resolve disputes between landlords/tenants, small businesses, and families without going to court.

Human Rights

Defense and advisory for individuals whose fundamental rights have been infringed upon.

Legal Awareness

Community sensitization programs on the rights of citizens and the Nigerian Constitution.

Become a Student Clinician

Are you a Law Student in 300L, 400L, or 500L? Join the clinic to gain practical interviewing, counseling, and drafting skills.

Requirements:

  • Must be in good academic standing (CGPA 3.0+).
  • Must pass the Ethics & Professional Responsibility interview.
  • Commitment of 5 hours per week.
